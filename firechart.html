Flame alphabet

Each flame in the fires that Dario speaks in corresponds to a letter (more accurately, a sound).
This fire can be decoded as a message using this chart on the right.

All vowel flames were sent by Bread when we picked them after catching a shiny jirachi in Pokemon Infinite Fusion.
They also said that there are 25 vowel sounds, 25 consonants from B to N, 20 consonants from P to Z, and 10 punctuation marks.

Since Bread had already given us most flames for catching every legendary in Infinite Fusion, they decided to give us all flames. The chart they gave us had 68 letters (mostly non-silent) and 9 punctuation flames (it's missing the dot flame).
