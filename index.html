HBC ARG documentation

Info

HalfBreadChaos' fire ARG spans across multiple videos and streams, in which an entity known as Dario or Arson communicates using fire (made up of ASCII art or sprites). Each flame corresponds to a different letter and pronounciation.
The ARG started on April 28th, 2023 and is currently in some kind of hiatus.
