Info

An ARG, or Alternate Reality Game, is a narrative that unfolds in real time by player involvement and may use multiple platforms (such as YouTube and Twitch) to tell its story.

The HalfBreadChaos ARG was created by YouTuber HalfBreadChaos. It spans across multiple videos and Twitch Streams, where an entity known as Dario speaks encoded messages through flame sprites made up of ASCII art, with each sprite corresponding with a sound. The ARG started on April 28th, 2023 and is prone to reset.
